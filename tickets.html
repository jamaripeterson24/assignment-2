Frequently Asked Questions

When can I use my ticket?

Single Day passes are valid for the selected date. Two-Day and Annual passes have flexible usage dates.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes! Tickets are transferable to another person, but Annual Passes are non-transferable and photo ID-based.

What's your refund policy?

Full refunds available up to 14 days before your visit. Partial refunds within 7 days of visit date.

Are there discounts available?

Yes! We offer discounts for military, teachers, AAA members, and advance bookings. Contact our team!

Is parking included?

Standard parking is $15/day. Free parking included with Annual Passes and Group Packages.

Can children under 3 visit free?

Yes! Children under 3 are free and don't need a ticket. They can enjoy most family-friendly attractions.
